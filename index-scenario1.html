Registered Traveller: get through the UK border faster

Registered Traveller membership gets you through border control faster.

You do not have to fill in a landing card (arrival card) and you can use the faster:

  • UK/European Union immigration lanes
  • 'ePassport' electronic gates, if you have a biometric ‘chip’ passport

You can use Registered Traveller at:

  • Heathrow
  • Gatwick
  • Manchester
  • Stansted
  • Luton
  • Edinburgh
  • Birmingham
  • Glasgow
  • East Midlands
  • London City

You also do not need to fill in a landing card at Eurostar train terminals at Paris, Brussels and Lille.

Eligibility: who can apply

You must:

  • be 18 or older
  • have a visa or have visited the UK at least 4 times in the last 24 months
  • have an eligible passport

Eligible passports

Australia, Canada, Hong Kong (must be a Special Administrative Region passport), Japan, New Zealand, South Korea, Singapore, Taiwan (must have your personal ID number on the photo page) or the USA.

How much it costs

It costs £70 to apply to use the service for 1 year. You’ll get £50 back if your application is unsuccessful.

How to apply

You need:

  • your passport number and expiry date
  • a debit or credit card to pay online

You will get a decision on your application within 10 working days.

If your application is accepted, the next time you travel to the UK you need to:

  • go through immigration control as normal
  • fill in a landing card

Start now

Existing members: updates and renewals

It costs:

  • £50 to renew your Registered Traveller membership for another year – we will email you 28 days before it expires
  • £20 to update your passport details if you have a new passport - you need to do this at least 5 days before you renew your membership

It doesn’t cost anything to update your visa information.
